About Us

Your Trusted Driving School in North London

Viva Driving School GB is a DVSA approved driving school based in North London, dedicated to helping learners become safe, skilled and confident drivers. Whether you're a complete beginner taking your very first lesson, or an experienced driver looking to brush up before your test, our friendly and patient instructors tailor every lesson to your needs.

With a strong focus on building real confidence behind the wheel, structured lesson plans and a relaxed teaching style, we've helped over 100 students pass their test — with a pass rate of around 90%. We offer both manual and automatic lessons, flexible scheduling, and intensive courses for those who want to get on the road fast.
